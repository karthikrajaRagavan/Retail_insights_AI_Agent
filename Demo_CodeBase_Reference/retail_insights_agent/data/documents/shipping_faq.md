# Shipping FAQ

## Delivery Times

### Standard Shipping
- Delivery: 5-7 business days
- Cost: Free on orders over $50, otherwise $5.99
- Available for all US addresses

### Express Shipping
- Delivery: 2-3 business days
- Cost: $12.99
- Order by 2 PM for same-day processing

### Next Day Delivery
- Delivery: 1 business day
- Cost: $24.99
- Order by 12 PM for next-day delivery
- Available only for select metro areas

### International Shipping
- Delivery: 10-15 business days
- Cost: Calculated at checkout based on destination
- Duties and taxes may apply

## Order Processing

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. During peak seasons (holidays, sales events), processing may take 3-4 business days.

Business days are Monday through Friday, excluding federal holidays.

## Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you will receive:
- Email confirmation with tracking number
- SMS updates (if opted in)
- Real-time tracking on our website

To track your order:
1. Go to Order History in your account
2. Click on the order number
3. Select "Track Package"

## Shipping Restrictions

We cannot ship to:
- PO Boxes (for orders over $100)
- APO/FPO addresses for certain items
- International destinations for hazardous materials

## Address Changes

You can modify your shipping address before the order ships:
1. Contact customer service immediately
2. Provide your order number
3. Give the new shipping address

Once an order has shipped, address changes are not possible.

## Missing or Lost Packages

If your package shows delivered but you haven't received it:

1. Check with neighbors and building management
2. Look for a delivery notice
3. Wait 24-48 hours as packages sometimes arrive late
4. Contact us with your order number

We will investigate and either resend or refund your order.

## Damaged Packages

If your package arrives damaged:
- Document the damage with photos
- Contact us within 48 hours
- Keep all packaging materials
- We will arrange replacement or refund

## Split Shipments

Large orders may be split into multiple shipments. This ensures faster delivery of in-stock items. You will only be charged once, and each shipment will have its own tracking number.

## Delivery Instructions

You can add special delivery instructions during checkout:
- Leave at door
- Require signature
- Deliver to back entrance
- Call upon arrival
