# Return Policy

## Overview

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we offer hassle-free returns within 30 days of delivery.

## Eligibility

Items eligible for return must meet the following criteria:

- Returned within 30 days of delivery date
- Item must be unused and in original packaging
- Tags must be attached
- Receipt or proof of purchase required

## Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

- Intimate apparel and swimwear
- Personalized or custom items
- Items marked as "Final Sale"
- Gift cards
- Perishable goods

## How to Return

1. Log into your account and go to Order History
2. Select the item you wish to return
3. Choose your reason for return
4. Print the prepaid shipping label
5. Pack the item securely and attach the label
6. Drop off at any authorized shipping location

## Refund Process

- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return
- Original payment method will be credited
- Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error
- For exchanges, the new item ships once we receive your return

## Store Credit Option

You may choose store credit instead of a refund. Store credit:

- Is issued immediately upon return approval
- Never expires
- Can be used on any purchase
- Includes a 10% bonus on credited amount

## Damaged or Defective Items

If you received a damaged or defective item:

- Contact customer service within 48 hours of delivery
- Provide photos of the damage
- We will arrange free return shipping
- Full refund including original shipping costs
