For the best fit, take measurements over undergarments.

## Care Instructions

### General Care Tips

- Always check the care label before washing
- Turn garments inside out before washing
- Wash similar colors together
- Use cold water to prevent shrinking and fading

### Fabric-Specific Care

**Cotton:**
- Machine wash cold or warm
- Tumble dry low
- Iron on medium heat if needed

**Silk:**
- Dry clean recommended
- If hand washing, use cold water and gentle detergent
- Do not wring or twist
- Air dry flat

**Wool:**
- Dry clean or hand wash cold
- Lay flat to dry
- Store folded, not hanging
- Use cedar blocks to prevent moths

**Synthetic fabrics (Polyester, Nylon):**
- Machine wash cold
- Tumble dry low
- Remove promptly to prevent wrinkles
- Do not use fabric softener
